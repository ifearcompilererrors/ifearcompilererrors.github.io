Jollibee on a Smoke Break at the End of the World (2024)

Coming off the high of rallying the Jollibee workers, the beloved mascot becomes overwhelmed with grief, ennui, and anxiety. Having been fired from his job as the corporate mascot of Jollibee, he must find new meaning in a world fixated on hyperconsumerism and corporate greed. He ditches his beloved sports car and wallows at the end of the world.

As seen on a billboard in Brooklyn. Curated by The Here and There Collective and SaveArtSpace.
